Netco

A Web app to simplify the college recruitment process.

We have often seen that students tend to ignore event posters put up all around college and as result of that, there is low turnout. Netco is a web application which aims to bridge the gap between students and the committees/events.

Whenever an event is around the corner, the committees can use this as a platform to publicize their event. Also, whenever a committee comes up with their recruitments, students can apply for the same by posting their SOPs on Netco and committees can take a look in advance before setting up the interview.

This will not only help the students applying for multiple committees but will also help the committees choose the best suited candidates very easily, and make each event a huge success.

About N.O.W.
Network for the Open Web was founded to serve as a platform for Internet Education. We strive to teach people about the uses of the Open Web, build Open Source projects and protect the Internet and its many users through the power of being Open.

We Teach Open Source. We Promote Open Source. We Build Open Source. We are Open Source.
